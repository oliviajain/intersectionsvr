ABOUT THE COMPANY

Intersections Virtual Reality was inspired by the need for diversity in the technology industry. It has been proven that diverse workforces lead to more creative and effective solutions. Currently, 20% of tech workers are women, __% are underrepresented minorities, and __% are persons with disabilities. Virtual reality has been proven to reduce unconscious bias by doing ___ and ____. IVR aims to create more diverse workplaces by opening people’s eyes to unconscious bias and discrimination in the workplace and thereby reducing oppression.Our goal is to address all types of unconscious racism, sexism, and other discrimination in the workplace through virtual simulation role-play scenarios.
